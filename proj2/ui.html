For this project we needed to create a unique design for an editorial website. I chose to re-design RIT's own DISTORTER (Reporter) website. We were required to design a desktop and mobile version, and then we prototyped the site. I used After Effects but we also learned how to use other applications such as Marvel, Adobe XD, and Invision.
